# Movement & Window

### Movement

* W, A, S, D move the player's point of view inside the map.
* Right and Left arrow rotate the player's point of view right and left respectively.&#x20;

### Window

* The use of use of the red cross on the window's frame of the press of the ESC key must close the window and cleanly quit the program.
* The management of the window must remain smooth: changing to another window, minimizing...
